# Writing Rules

## Purpose
This file keeps portfolio writing consistent across home, work, about, and contact sections.

## Voice
- personal but not sloppy
- concise
- confident without overclaiming
- warm, slightly editorial
- clear before clever

## Headline rules
- communicate identity quickly
- avoid vague slogans
- one clear primary idea per headline

## Project card copy
- title first
- category/tags second
- optional teaser short and specific
- avoid long marketing language

## Case study writing
- explain context, role, decisions, and outcome
- prefer specifics over generic “I improved UX” claims
- show what changed and why

## About writing
- make the person feel memorable
- keep it human and grounded
- avoid long autobiography unless it adds meaning

## Contact writing
- direct and frictionless
- do not bury email/social paths

## Copy restraint
If a sentence sounds decorative but adds no information, cut it.
